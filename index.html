Explore Luxury Hotels around the Globe

The Whitby Hotel

New York

Situated in upper midtown Manhattan, 483 metres from the Central Park, the Whitby Hotel is a short distance away from stores such as Saks Fifth Avenue and Bergdorf Goodman. The Whitby Hotel offers complimentary WiFi to all guests.

Hôtel National Des Arts et Métiers

Paris

Situated within 600 metres of Pompidou Centre and 800 metres of Place de la République, Hôtel National Des Arts et Métiers features a restaurant and a bar in Paris. Free WiFi is available throughout the hotel.

Santiago de Alfama - Boutique Hotel

Lisbon

The 5-star Santiago de Alfama – Boutique Hotel is located in the historic centre of Lisbon just an 8-minute walk from the iconic São Jorge Castle and offers panoramic views of the city. With free WiFi, this charming hotel is just 500 metres from the Pombaline Downtown.

Sofitel Chicago Magnificent Mile

Chicago

Just 2 blocks away from the famed shopping, dining and entertainment on the Magnificent Mile, this luxury Chicago Gold Coast hotel is housed in a 32-floor prism glass building featuring stunning views of Lake Michigan and the city skyline. Adorned in French art de vivre furnishings and décor, each room at Sofitel Chicago Magnificent Mile features luxury feathertop duvet bedding and a spacious marble bathroom.

ME Dubai by Meliá

Dubai

Located in Dubai, 1.7 km from The Dubai Fountain, ME Dubai by Meliá was designed by Zaha Hadid and offers a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre. Each accommodation at the 5-star hotel has city views, and guests can enjoy access to a bar and to a terrace.

Fairmont Grand Del Mar

San Diego

Featuring 4 outdoor pools, this San Diego 5-diamond, Mediterranean-inspired resort features tennis courts, 6 restaurants, a lobby bar, a golf course and a spa. Plush bathrobes and free WiFi is provided in all rooms.

Silver Lake Pool & Inn

Los Angeles

Silver Lake Pool & Inn has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a bar and garden in Los Angeles. Among the facilities at this property are a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property.

Delano Las Vegas at Mandalay Bay

Las Vegas

Located directly on the Las Vegas Strip, this all-suite tower is part of the Mandalay Bay Hotel. It features a full-service spa, a shark aquarium in Mandalay Bay, and luxurious suites with cable TV. Exclusive to Delano Las Vegas guests, Delano Beach Club is a luxurious pool club with personalised services.
